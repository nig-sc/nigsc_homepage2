## FireDucks Enterprise Now Available

2026.04.26

FireDucks is a pandas-compatible high-performance dataframe library developed by NEC. Thanks to the generosity of NEC, the Enterprise edition of FireDucks — enhanced with features tailored for bioinformatics — is available free of charge on the NIG Supercomputer System. Please read and agree to the End User License Agreement before use.

For usage instructions, please refer to the [🔗 FireDucks Enterprise page](/advanced_guides/FireDucks/).

## Introducing Amelieff Inc.'s BI Practical Lab — a resource for making the most of the NIG Supercomputer

2026.04.26

As the volume of data in life science research continues to grow explosively, the use of supercomputers as large-scale computational resources has become indispensable. For those of you performing bioinformatics analyses on the supercomputer, we would like to introduce a free learning site that can help you develop your analytical skills and troubleshoot day-to-day issues.
