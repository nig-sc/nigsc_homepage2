## 1. 13–19 March 2026: Scheduled Maintenance

In connection with the statutory power outage at NIG on Saturday, 14 March 2026, scheduled maintenance of the NIG Supercomputer will be carried out from 13 March to 19 March 2026.

The NIG Supercomputer will not be available during this period.

Any running jobs will be removed during this period. Users are kindly asked to resubmit their jobs once maintenance has been completed.

Further details are available at:

[[Maintenance] 13–19 March 2026:  Maintenance](/en/blog/2026-03-13-scheduled-maintenance/)

## 2. Notice on Billing for the Third Period of 2025


Applications for new subscriptions to the third period of the paid service, and any requests for amendment, closed on 31 December 2025.

From 1 January 2026, changes involving any alteration to fees are no longer possible under the third period of the paid service.

The expected invoice amount has already been communicated to users. Invoices will be issued in mid-March.

Further information is available at:

https://sc.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/en/application/invoice/
